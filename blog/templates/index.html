SHARE YOUR VIEW!


NOW EVERYONE CAN WRITE AN OPINION-EDITORIAL!

The Constitution of India guarantees everyone a Right to Freedom of Speech. So exercise it! We often read op-editorials in newspapers where the authors express their views on current affairs. Often, the view could be biased - either too left or too right - which you are not comfortable with. You may have an opinion of your own but not a good space to express it. Your search is over! It's Your View is exactly the place where you should be. We believe everyone can write, and it need not be on just the current affairs. Share your knowledge on history, economy, science & technology, travel, Bollywood - literally on anything in the world so that others can read it and you can become a great writer!


WHY USE It's Your View?

  •   Anyone can read and write free of cost
  •   No hassles of license for authors
  •   One click submission of article - no need to mail and get an approval

MEET OUR TEAM:


Vishal Khopkar

CEO, Editor

In today's world where news spreads faster than ever, everyone holds opinions on different topics. Some are right-wingers, some are socialists while some detest masculine authority. There is a debate and counter-debate on these opinions all the time and everywhere - at home, office, college, etc. Everyone doesn't need to support any stencil opinion. You will find some people staunchly supporting the issue whereas some support with various limitations. These opinions should come out to the world - and Facebook is not the right place to write several paragraphs in one post. I used to write blogs for Times of India, but at one time they became unresponsive and rejected my article for no reason. That's when I realised that India needs a platform where writers can write opinion editorials, as they appear in the newspaper, without being at anyone's mercy. Thus, this platform was born.
