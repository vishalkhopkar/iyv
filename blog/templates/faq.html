An op-ed (opposite the editorial or opinion-editorial) is a section that appears usually in the middle of a newspaper. It is widely read by many avid newspaper readers. It contains the views and opinions of journalists or experts. Their opinion is backed by facts and figures. They are of a special interest to students preparing for government competitive exams like the UPSC and state PSCs civil services examinations. The topics on which they are usually written pertain to current affairs, but can also be written to remember history on specific days. For more information, please refer to Op-ed - Wikipedia.
